# Final Judge Testing Instructions

Project URL:
https://trailsense-lebanon.grazsal.chatgpt.site

Access:
No account, payment, installation, GPX upload, or location permission is required.

Recommended testing flow:

1. Open the project URL.
2. Click “Try the Hackathon Demo.”
3. Review the Daily Hike Recommendations.
4. Open the Best Match.
5. Review the trail map, elevation, fatigue, weather, and daylight analysis.
6. Click “Continue to Live Hike,” then “Start Simulated Live Hike.”
7. Choose “Slower Than Expected · 23%.”
8. Observe how Trail-Intel recalculates pace, finish time, future risks, and daylight margin.
9. Complete the simulation to view the post-hike summary.

Optional:
Run the “Off-Route Deviation” simulation to test route-deviation detection and recovery.

Location permission:
Not required for the simulated demo.

Demo data:
The catalogue includes curated Lebanon and United States trails. The guided scenario uses seeded forecast evidence to remain reliable during judging.

Cost:
The project is available free of charge throughout the judging period.

Known limitations:
Live sessions are held in memory and can reset when the service restarts. The simulated judge flow uses seeded conditions rather than live weather or GPS. Browser background GPS is not guaranteed. Trail-Intel provides decision support, not safety certification, rescue service, or a guarantee of trail conditions.
