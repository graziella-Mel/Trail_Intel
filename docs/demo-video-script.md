# Demo video script (2–3 minutes)

1. Open `/demo`. Explain that the inputs and forecast fallback are seeded for repeatability, while ranking uses the production deterministic scorer.
2. Click **Rank Today’s Trails**. Compare the best match with the easier and challenging alternatives using score components, finish time, daylight, weather, and concerns.
3. Open the best trail. Briefly show its fatigue-colored route, elevation/effort chart, direction, and personal timing.
4. Open Live Hike Mode. Choose **Slower Than Expected · 23%** and click **Start Simulated Live Hike**.
5. Point out live progress, actual versus planned pace, the later predicted finish, shrinking daylight margin, future difficult section, and structured recommendation.
6. Let the replay complete and show actual/moving/stopped time, deviation and warning count, plus the opt-in profile-calibration suggestion.
7. Close with the limitation: Trail-Intel supports decisions; it does not certify trail safety or replace emergency preparation.
